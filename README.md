# BD_Cricket-Chatbot
BCB-Info-Bot is an intent-based chatbot designed to serve as the ultimate digital guide for Bangladesh cricket fans. It covers the complete history of the Tigers from their first World Cup in 1999 to the present day (2025).

Key Features:
Historical Archive: Detailed info on major milestones, including the 1999 World Cup, 2000 Inaugural Test, and 2005 first major wins.

Current Intel: Up-to-date 2025 rankings, coaching staff, and captains (Split Captaincy).

Tournament Squads: Rosters for the Champions Trophy 2025, Asia Cup, and bilateral series.

Player Profiles: Comprehensive stats for legends (Shakib, Tamim, Mashrafe) and rising stars (Hridoy, Rishad).

Match Results: Recent performance summaries for Tests, ODIs, and T20Is.
